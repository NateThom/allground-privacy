1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Email address (required for account creation)
  • Name (required)
  • Password (stored securely, never transmitted in plain text)

Preferences:

  • Push notification preferences (opt-in)
  • App settings

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Device Information:

  • Device model and operating system
  • App version
  • Unique device identifiers (for push notifications only)

Usage Data:

  • App launch and interaction logs
  • Push notification delivery status
  • Error and crash reports (anonymous)

1.3 Information Not Collected

We do not collect:

  • Precise geolocation data
  • Photos or media from your device (except images you upload for announcements if you are an admin)
  • Contact lists or phonebook data
  • Social media data
  • Payment information (the app is free)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

2.1 To Provide the App's Core Functionality

  • Create and authenticate your account
  • Display announcements from AllGround Jiu Jitsu
  • Send push notifications (with your consent)
  • Enable administrative features (for admin users only)

2.2 To Improve the App

  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve performance
  • Debug errors and crashes
  • Monitor service uptime and reliability

2.3 To Communicate with You

  • Send important updates about the app or service
  • Respond to support requests
  • Notify you about changes to this Privacy Policy

2.4 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to operate the App. These providers have access to your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated to protect it:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, file storage, and push notifications
    • Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Expo (Expo.dev): App infrastructure and over-the-air updates
    • Expo Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as to:

  • Comply with a subpoena, court order, or legal process
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of AllGround, our users, or others
  • Investigate fraud or security issues

3.3 Business Transfers

If AllGround is acquired by or merged with another entity, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or in-app notification if this occurs.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

4.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encryption at Rest: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt; sensitive data is encrypted in Firebase
  • Access Controls: Administrative features require authentication and role-based permissions

4.2 Physical and Administrative Safeguards

  • Firebase infrastructure is hosted in secure, SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certified data centers
  • Access to production systems is limited to authorized personnel
  • We conduct regular security audits of our codebase and infrastructure

4.3 Limitations

While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. If you become aware of any unauthorized access to your account, please contact us immediately at support@allgroundjiujitsu.com.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the App's services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Announcements: Retained indefinitely or until deleted by an admin
  • Logs and Crash Reports: Retained for up to 90 days
  • Push Notification Tokens: Retained while your account is active or until you disable notifications

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain it (e.g., for tax or legal compliance).

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

6.1 Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Receive your data in a machine-readable format

6.2 Correction and Deletion

  • Update or correct inaccurate information in your account settings
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

6.3 Objection and Restriction

  • Object to certain processing of your data
  • Request that we restrict how we use your data

6.4 Withdraw Consent

  • Disable push notifications at any time via app settings
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

6.5 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@allgroundjiujitsu.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Children's Privacy

The AllGround App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at support@allgroundjiujitsu.com, and we will delete it promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including the United States (where Firebase servers are located). We ensure that any international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws, such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EEA users
  • Adequate safeguards as required by GDPR, CCPA, and other regulations

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you via push notification or email (for material changes)
  • Post the updated policy in the App and on our website

Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
